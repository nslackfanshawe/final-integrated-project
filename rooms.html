Room Styles

Welcome to the rooms available for your stay at Couples Resort. While each room has its own unique luxuries each include access to these magnificent universal benefits. Every style comes with access to a delicious 5 course dinner and breakfast in bed at no extra charge guaranteeing no one goes hungry. Each spacious lakeside suite exceeds 5 Diamond standards and features a private outdoor hot tub, walkout balcony or deck, and a king-size bed with fine linens. Enjoy the warmth of a wood-burning fireplace, a relaxing two-person whirlpool bath, and modern touches such as a 50-inch television, fibre-optic WiFi, and a European shower with multiple heads. Alongside these are the staples you would expect in any luxury room such as 2 bath robes, a hairdryer and cosmetic mirror, irons and an ironing board, a Keurig coffee maker with K-cups, a bar fridge, free long-distance phones, 2 chaise lounge chairs, safes, an alarm clock, and access to clean fresh tap water sources from the Algonquin Park aquifer.
