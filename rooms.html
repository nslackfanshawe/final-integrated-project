Room Styles

Welcome to the rooms available for your stay at Couples Resort. While each room has its own unique luxuries each include access to these magnificent universal benefits. Every style comes with access to a delicious 5 course dinner and breakfast in bed at no extra charge guaranteeing no one goes hungry. Each spacious lakeside suite exceeds 5 Diamond standards and features a private outdoor hot tub, walkout balcony or deck, and a king-size bed with fine linens. Enjoy the warmth of a wood-burning fireplace, a relaxing two-person whirlpool bath, and modern touches such as a 50-inch television, fibre-optic WiFi, and a European shower with multiple heads. Alongside these are the staples you would expect in any luxury room such as 2 bath robes, a hairdryer and cosmetic mirror, irons and an ironing board, a Keurig coffee maker with K-cups, a bar fridge, free long-distance phones, 2 chaise lounge chairs, safes, an alarm clock, and access to clean fresh tap water sources from the Algonquin Park aquifer.

With all of these universal amenities no matter which room style you end up staying at will be a wonderful experience.

Couples Resort
Chateaus

The chateau is the most luxurious of all the room styles coming with many extra not offered to any other style. The chateau is the largest of our rooms coming in at a spacious 1400 sq feet, we guarantee you wont feel cramped in this style. This style grants you access to a private outdoor hot tub perfect for warming yourself on chilly winter nights. Your room comes equipped with a war in-built sauna and a steam generator for the double shower that comes with 4 shower heads and benches. Last of the tools that are offered in the chateau are a bidet and a microwave for conveniance. With your stay you get a free basket of lotions, bath salts, and specialty products, free soapstone products by Bath and Bodyworks, and free Illy Espresso / Americano Machine with caps. This comes with all of the basic amenities afforded to every room. With the chateau you wont just remember the adventures during your stay you'll remember the room as well.
